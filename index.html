About Us

KOJOBS UW's purpose is to provide career related resources and opportunities in the U.S. and South Korea, to build fruitful relationship between upperclassmen and underclassmen of all majors within the university, and to continuously network with professionals in the Greater Seattle Area.

Events

We host events to help UW students gain resources for both academic success and career opportunities.

From our annual KONNECT event for underclassmen to learn about various majors in the university to corporate seminars where students receive advices from professionals working in Amazon, Google, Meta, and more, we seek to host events that could give both practical benefits and enjoy a fun campus life meeting new people.
