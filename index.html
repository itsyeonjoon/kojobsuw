About Us

KOJOBS UW's purpose is to provide career-related resources and opportunities in the U.S. and South Korea, to build fruitful relationship between upperclassmen and underclassmen of all majors within the university, and to continuously network with professionals in the Greater Seattle Area.

Events

Evens are hosted to help UW students gain resources for both academic and career success.

Examples include the annual KONNECT event where a mentoring system is offered to learn about various majors within UW, as well as Corporate Seminars where current employees from Amazon,Google, Meta, etc. are invited to share their expertise and advice. Additional events are hosted to give practical benefits while allowing students to engage in a fun campus life while meeting new people.
