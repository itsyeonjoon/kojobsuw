Events

KO:NNECT

  • Mentoring program that matches pre-major underclassmen with upperclassmen in the desired major. Professionally organized event to assist students with academic and career guidance while allowing to network with peer students in the same field.

General Meeting

  • Introduction to our student organization at the beginning of the school year. Presented our visions and goals, upcoming events, organizational structure, and officer members.

Corporate Seminar

  • Invited a panel of UW alumni and professionals from the Greater Seattle Area, including Amazon, Google, Meta, PwC, and UW Dental School, to provide career guidance as well as practical advice and counseling. Offered opportunities for resume assistance from the professionals.

Networking Night

  • Collaborative networking event to allow students to connect with peer colleagues within the same major. First half of the event consisted of collaborative activities and games to allow relationship bonding. Second half of the event consisted of academic and career-related conversations to exchange beneficial tips and information.
